Pricing

The Pricing screen is used to price the portfolio. Prices for instruments, such as equities, options, preferreds, and futures, that trade on the exchange are sourced from IDC. Prices for bonds and other structured products are sourced via an external evaluated pricing service. Other instruments like bank debt, trade claims do not have a pricing feed and have to be priced manually. The pricing screen workflow involves ensuring all prices received from the evaluated pricing source are correct and prices for instruments that are not available via the evaluated pricing source are inputted.

Filter

Users can filter their portfolio by using the filtering panel. The filter panel can be accessed via the funnel icon. The filter panel loads with the most recent pricing date and the second most recent pricing date (previous pricing date). The Price column and the Previous Price column in the porfolio grid refer to the prices as of Pricing Date and Previous Pricing date respectively. The Change column in the difference between the two. Users can change the pricing and the previous picing date from the filter panel. There is a Carry Over button which copies prices from previous date to pricing date for the selected asset type. On clicking carry over button, it will ask for a confirmation(cancel & ok) to make sure user has not accidently click the button. Upon clicking ok, it will perform the carry over task.

Pricing Panel

This panel is used to add/delete prices for instruments that are either priced via an evaluated pricing source or not priced at all via an evaluated pricing source. Users can input multiple prices received from multiple sources. The average of the prices that are “checked” is used as the final price for the instrument.

Price History

Users can see price history of an instrument and compare it to price history of other instruments, both in a chart format as well, as excel via the Action button.
  1. At the bottom of the price comparison chart, there is another graph called Focus graph which shows the overview of the main graph. It also lets you to zoom in to view graph for particular dates just by clicking and dragging it.
  2. There is a Download Excel button at top-right which lets you download the data of graph in excel format.
