Reports

This screen is used to view the reports. A user can slice and dice the data by applying grouping, sorting and filtering on report columns, and also can save his own custom reports. User can also export these reports in excel or pdf formats.

Add or remove fields to report

  1. Click on the report you want to modify
  2. Click on the cog-icon
  3. Add fields from available items and remove from current items

Applying filters

  1. Click on the report you want to filter
  2. Click on the funel-icon
  3. Add values for appropriate columns to filter

Applying grouping

  1. Open a report
  2. Hover on the column on which you want to apply grouping
  3. Click on the tripple-bar-icon
  4. Click on the "Group By column-name"

Creating custom report

  1. Apply configuration on any report
  2. Click on file-icon
  3. Click on "Save-as"
  4. Enter the name for custom report and click save

Export Reports

  1. Click on the report you want to export
  2. Click on vector-icon
  3. Click on "PDF" or "XLS" and report will be downloaded

Rename custom report

  1. Click on the custom report you want to rename
  2. Hover on the report name
  3. Click on pencil-icon
  4. Rename the report and click on save
